Carousel creates a carousel similar to bootstrap's image carousel. The carousel also offers support for touchscreen devices in the form of swiping. To enable swiping, load the ngTouch module as a dependency. Use a <carousel> element with <slide> elements inside it. It will automatically cycle through the slides at a given rate, and a current-index variable will be kept in sync with the currently visible slide.
